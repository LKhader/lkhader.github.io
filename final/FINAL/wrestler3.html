Fighter Info

Name: Louie "Big Round" Sirgiovanni

Height: 5'10

Age: 23

Stats: 12 - 9

Weight 250 lbs.

Fun Fact

Louie is a Puerto Rican who comes from humble beginnings. Growing up in Chicago he realized from a young age he was destined to become a star. He struggled with school and began to wrestle for fun on his free time. This slowly turned into his passion, after years of dedication he has now been able to elevate his game into the world reknown Big Round Sirgiovanni.
